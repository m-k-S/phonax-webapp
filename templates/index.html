Phonax

Phonax is a software tool that makes predictions of the vibrational and phonon modes of molecules and periodic crystals.
It is orders of magnitude faster than ab-initio phonon calculation frameworks such as DFPT or GWPT.
This webapp is intended to serve as a convenient interface for quickly calculating and plotting phonon band structure and density of states.
To use it, simply upload a CIF file for a valid crystal structure.
Phonax was written by Shiang Fang and Mario Geiger.
